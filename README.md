## The ingredients (a.k.a. tools) for your productive life, powered by local-first AI, and wrapped up in one convenient, private, on-the-go package. Keep your data fresh, local, and private with Burrito Productivity.

Unlike traditional apps that send your schedule, notes, and emails to the cloud, Burrito keeps your data where it belongs: **on your device.** Using advanced **Edge AI** and a multi-agent orchestration layer, Burrito provides deep intelligence without sending your data around the internet.

## üî¨ Local AI Architecture

Burrito uses a unique **Hub vs. Brain** separation of concerns.

1. **The Hub:** The Kotlin "Operating System" that holds your context, manages the sandbox, and executes tools via MCP.
2. **The Brain:** Specialized models (1B and ~3B) that handle Intent Routing, Reasoning, and Logic Auditing (Criticism).
